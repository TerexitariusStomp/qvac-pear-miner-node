## Distributed Systems Consensus

### What is Consensus?

Consensus is the process by which a group of individuals or entities agree on a particular goal or decision. In the context of distributed systems, consensus refers to the agreement among multiple nodes (computers) on a set of rules or values. This agreement is crucial for maintaining the integrity and reliability of the system.
